About Little Spoon

Little Spoon is a Graphic Design, Print, and Marketing business founded by Lucy Tasker

Lucy has qualifications in Graphic Design, Photography and Marketing, and has years of work experience in these fields. Little Spoon caters to your needs as an individual or business, from business cards to tshirts and everything in between.

Originally from the UK, Lucy moved to New Zealand 4 years ago, and is currently based in Ohakune, but caters to New Zealand as a whole.
